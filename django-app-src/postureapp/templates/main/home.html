Following is some guidance and tips on the app's usage


The Sitting Posture App helps you improve your posture by monitoring your sitting position and providing real-time feedback. Here's how it works:

  1. The app is connected to a Jetson Nano, which uses its camera to monitor your sitting posture.
  2. You'll need to authenticate with the Jetson Nano to start monitoring your posture.
  3. The app detects incorrect postures (Back Forward, Back Reclined, Neck Forward) in segements of 10 seconds.
  4. The app will send you a notification in real-time if it detects that you're slouching or adopting a poor posture.
  5. You can then adjust your posture as needed to maintain good sitting posture and avoid discomfort or pain.

Note: In order to use this app, you'll need a Jetson Nano with a camera connected to it. Make sure to authenticate with the Jetson Nano before using the app to monitor your posture.
